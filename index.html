Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, Punjab

Established in 2005, Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university located in Jalandhar, Punjab. Lovely Professional University is approved by UGC, UGC-DEB, NCTE, PCI, BCI, COA, PSCAE, ITPI, Indian Association of Physiotherapists, and AICTE. LPU University is accredited by ACBSP, ICAR, and with A ++ Grade by NAAC. LPU Punjab is a member of the AIU, IAU, IIE, The Institution of Engineers (India), and ACU. LPU is famous for its flagship courses which are BTech and MTech..

F.A.Q

  • How can I register for the conference?

    You can register for the conference by visiting our conference website and clicking on the "Get Pass". Follow the instructions provided, and make sure to complete all required fields. You'll receive a confirmation email once your registration is successful.

  • What is the conference schedule, and how can I access it?

    The conference schedule will be available on our conference website. You can access it by clicking on the "Event Schedule" tab. The schedule will include information about keynote speakers, sessions, workshops, and social events.

  • Will there be networking opportunities at the conference?

    Yes, the conference typically includes networking sessions, such as receptions, coffee breaks, and social events. These opportunities allow attendees to connect with fellow participants, speakers, and sponsors.

  • How can I obtain a certificate of attendance or participation?

    Yes, Certificates will be provided to everyone after the completion of the Conference. The certificates will be sent to your respesctive emails.This may take few weeks time.

  • Can I request a refund if I can't attend the conference after registering?

    Refund policies vary, so it's important to review the conference's cancellation and refund policy, which is usually available on the registration page. Many conferences offer partial or full refunds if you cancel within a specified timeframe.

  • What is the dress code for the conference?

    The dress code is typically business casual. You may want to dress comfortably while maintaining a professional appearance. Please check the conference website or any specific guidelines sent to your mail for any variations in the dress code.
